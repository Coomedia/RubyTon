About us

RubyTon is a basket full of goodness. Started in 2022 by a vibrant couple Mr. Anthony & Ms. Ruby, their main aim being bringing disney world right to the neighborhood.

With high spirits and a lot of determination, the founders gathered themselves together, bought a few stalls and began the dream of bringing happiness to their community as their way of giving back to the community with the slogan “You need it we've got it”.

They started slowly with a game hub for the youth to enjoy themselves by passing time gaming instead of engaging in questionable practices, a cyber cafe to help those who need digital services like printing, typing out documents and such exercises, a studio for people looking to keep a significant memory in their lives in a photo held within the cyber cafe, a bookshop to satisfy the average student’s needs whether it’s new books or a mathematical set, to their newest addition, a baby shop to get something nice for your little bundle of joy.

The founders have tried their level best to make the RubyTon Disneyworld experience one to behold and one to remember.
